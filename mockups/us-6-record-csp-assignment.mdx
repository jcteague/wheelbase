_As a wheel trader managing an open cash-secured put, I want to manually record when my CSP has been assigned, so that my position accurately reflects that I now hold shares and my cost basis captures the premium already collected._

## Acceptance Criteria States

- **form**: Sheet open with date "2026-01-17"; summary card shows premium waterfall ($180.00 − $3.50 = $176.50)
- **validation-error**: Submitted without a date — red inline error "Assignment date is required"

- **future-date-warning**: Date in the future — gold soft warning "This date is in the future — are you sure?"; form still submittable
- **success**: Hero card "HOLDING 100 SHARES · $176.50/share"; strategic nudge before CC CTA

                    error={dateError}
                    hint={dateHint}
                  >
                    <MockDatePicker
                      value={pickerValue}
                      hasError={isDateError || isDateBeforeOpen}
                      onChange={setDateValue}
                    />
                  </MockField>

                  {/* Future date warning — Real: <AlertBox variant="warning">This date is in the future…</AlertBox> */}
                  {isFutureWarning && (
                    <div style={{ marginTop: 8 }}>
                      <MockAlertBox variant="warning">
                        This date is in the future — are you sure?
                      </MockAlertBox>
                    </div>
                  )}
                </div>

                {/* Irrevocable warning — Real: <AlertBox variant="warning"><strong>This cannot be undone.</strong>…</AlertBox> */}
                <div style={{ marginTop: 16 }}>
                  <MockAlertBox variant="warning">
                    <strong style={{ fontWeight: 700, display: 'block', marginBottom: 3 }}>This cannot be undone.</strong>
                    The position will transition to Holding Shares. Full leg history is preserved.
                  </MockAlertBox>
                </div>
              </>
            )}
          </div>

          {/* ── Footer ── */}
          {!isSuccess && (
            <div style={{
              padding: '16px 24px',
              borderTop: '1px solid var(--wb-border, #1e2a38)',
              display: 'flex',
              gap: 10,
              flexShrink: 0,
              background: 'var(--wb-bg-surface, #070a0e)',
            }}>
              {/* Real: <Button variant="outline" onClick={onClose} style={{ flex: 1 }}>Cancel</Button> */}
              <MockOutlineButton style={{ flex: 1 }}>Cancel</MockOutlineButton>
              {/* Real: <FormButton label="Confirm Assignment" pendingLabel="Confirming…" isPending={isPending} style={{ flex: 1 }} /> */}
              <MockFormButton
                label="Confirm Assignment"
                onClick={() => setState('success')}
                style={{ flex: 1, fontWeight: 700 }}
              />
            </div>
          )}

        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  )
}
