# US-10: Record Shares Called Away

_As a wheel trader whose covered call has been exercised, I want to record that my shares were called away at the CC strike, so that the wheel cycle completes with an accurate final P&L that accounts for all premiums collected and the share appreciation from cost basis to strike._

## Acceptance Criteria States

- **confirmation**: Sheet open showing CC details; P&L waterfall (CC strike − effective cost basis × shares + total premiums = final P&L); fill date auto-derived from CC (read-only); irrevocable warning; Confirm / Cancel footer
- **success**: "WHEEL COMPLETE" hero card with final P&L (+$780.00) displayed prominently in green; cycle summary card with per-component breakdown; "Start New Wheel on AAPL →" CTA

                      label={<span style={{ fontSize: 10, color: 'var(--wb-text-muted, #4a5a6a)' }}>effective cost basis · {pos.sharesHeld} shares</span>}
                      value={'$' + pos.basisPerShare + '/share · $' + (parseFloat(pos.basisPerShare) * pos.sharesHeld).toFixed(2) + ' total'}
                    />
                  </MockSectionCard>
                </div>

                {/* Fill date — read-only, auto-derived from CC_OPEN leg
                  Real:
                    <Field label="Fill Date (CC Expiration)" hint="Derived from your CC — the day shares are delivered">
                      <Input readOnly value={ccExpiration} />
                    </Field>
                  The fill price is always the CC strike; the trader does not enter it manually.
                */}
                <div style={{ marginBottom: 6 }}>
                  <MockReadOnlyField
                    label="Fill Date"
                    value={pos.ccExpiration}
                    hint="Derived from your CC — the day shares are delivered to the buyer"
                  />
                </div>

                {/* Irrevocable warning — Real: <AlertBox variant="warning">…</AlertBox> */}
                <div style={{ marginTop: 16 }}>
                  <MockAlertBox variant="warning">
                    <strong style={{ fontWeight: 700, display: 'block', marginBottom: 3 }}>This cannot be undone.</strong>
                    The position will close as WHEEL_COMPLETE. Full leg history is preserved.
                  </MockAlertBox>
                </div>
              </>
            )}
          </div>

          {/* ── Footer ── */}
          {!isSuccess && (
            <div style={{
              padding: '16px 24px',
              borderTop: '1px solid var(--wb-border, #1e2a38)',
              display: 'flex',
              gap: 10,
              flexShrink: 0,
              background: 'var(--wb-bg-surface, #070a0e)',
            }}>
              {/* Real: <Button variant="outline" onClick={onClose} style={{ flex: 1 }}>Cancel</Button> */}
              <MockOutlineButton style={{ flex: 1 }}>Cancel</MockOutlineButton>
              {/* Real: <FormButton label="Confirm Call-Away" pendingLabel="Confirming…" isPending={isPending} style={{ flex: 1 }} /> */}
              <MockFormButton
                label="Confirm Call-Away"
                onClick={() => setState('success')}
                style={{ flex: 1, fontWeight: 700 }}
              />
            </div>
          )}

        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  )
}
