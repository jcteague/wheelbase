# US-16: Cost Basis After Sequential Rolls

_The cost basis engine correctly computes basis after multiple sequential rolls — including strike-changing rolls where the delta is larger than just the net credit._

## Acceptance Criteria States

- **Snapshot Chain**: Full audit trail of 6 cost basis snapshots across the wheel lifecycle. Row 3 (down-roll) shows the basis drop of −$3.30, not just −$0.30, because the strike reduction is included in the formula.
- **Formula Comparison**: Side-by-side comparison of the two calculation paths — same-strike roll (simple subtraction) vs. different-strike CSP roll (must include strike delta in the formula).
